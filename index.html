Celebrating Literature with Uganda Bookshop

Since 1927, Uganda Bookshop has been a cornerstone in nurturing the culture of reading and learning in the community. With a rich history of supporting local authors and providing a wide range of books, we stand as a beacon of knowledge for every reader.


Dr. Travor James, a celebrated author and advocate for education, has been a pivotal figure in our journey. His works, emphasizing the importance of literacy and education in Uganda, resonate with our mission. Through collaboration, we aim to continue enriching lives by making literature accessible to all.
